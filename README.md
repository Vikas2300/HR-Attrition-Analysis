# HR-Attrition-Analysis
1. 👥 HR Attrition Analysis Dashboard: Employee Retention & Workforce Insights

A dynamic and interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze employee attrition patterns, workforce demographics, performance trends, and departmental turnover. The dashboard helps HR professionals identify retention challenges and make data-driven workforce decisions.

2. The HR Attrition Analysis Dashboard is a comprehensive Power BI report developed to monitor employee turnover, analyze workforce demographics, and evaluate employee performance across departments. It enables HR teams and business leaders to identify attrition drivers, improve employee retention strategies, and optimize workforce planning.

3. The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

• 📊 Power BI Desktop – Main platform used for dashboard development and data visualization.

• 📂 Power Query – Used for data cleaning, transformation, and preparation.

• 🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Used to create KPIs, calculated measures, and business metrics such as Employee Churn Rate.

• 🔗 Data Modeling – Established relationships between Employee, Performance, and Action tables to enable efficient cross-filtering and analysis.

• 📈 Interactive Visualizations – Cards, Gauges, Pie Charts, Bar Charts, Area Charts, Line Charts, and Slicers.

• 📁 File Format – .pbix/.pbit for development and reporting.

4. Source:

HR Management System (Sample HR Dataset)

The dataset contains employee-related information including:

Employee ID
Department Details
Gender
Employee Performance Ratings
Employee Actions (Resignation, Termination, Transfer, etc.)
Effective Dates
Workforce Demographics
Employee Retention Metrics

The data is structured across multiple interconnected tables:

Employee Table
Performance Table
Action Table

These tables are linked through Employee IDs to enable comprehensive workforce analysis.

5. Employee attrition is one of the most critical challenges faced by organizations. High turnover can lead to:

Increased recruitment and training costs
Loss of organizational knowledge
Reduced productivity
Lower employee morale

Organizations often struggle to identify:

Which departments experience the highest turnover?
What employee groups are most affected?
How does performance relate to retention?
Are attrition trends increasing over time?

Answering these questions manually from HR records is time-consuming and inefficient.

• Goal of the Dashboard

The dashboard was designed to:

Monitor workforce retention and attrition metrics.
Track employee churn across departments.
Analyze workforce demographics and diversity.
Evaluate employee performance trends.
Support strategic HR decision-making.
Improve employee retention initiatives through data-driven insights.

• Walkthrough of Key Visuals
Key KPIs (Top Section)

Displays critical HR metrics such as:

Total Employees
Employee Churn Rate (%)
Overall Employee Performance Rating
These KPIs provide an instant snapshot of workforce health.
Employee Distribution by Department (Column Chart)
Shows the number of employees across various departments.
Helps HR teams understand workforce allocation and identify departments with larger employee populations.
Employee Performance Trend (Line Chart)
Tracks employee performance ratings over time.
Enables management to observe performance patterns and workforce productivity trends.
Gender Distribution (Pie Chart)
Visual representation of employee distribution by gender.
Supports diversity and inclusion analysis within the organization.
Employee Actions Analysis (Donut Chart)

Displays different employee actions such as:
Resignation
Termination
Transfers
Other workforce movements

Helps identify the most common workforce events impacting employee retention.
Attrition Trend Over Time (Area Chart)
Analyzes employee action frequency over different time periods.
Allows HR managers to identify seasonal or recurring attrition patterns.
Department Performance Comparison (Bar Chart)
Compares average employee performance ratings across departments.
Highlights high-performing departments and areas requiring improvement.
Workforce Size Indicator (Gauge Chart)
Provides a visual representation of total employee count against organizational benchmarks.
Offers quick insight into workforce capacity.
Interactive Filters (Slicers)
Users can filter the dashboard by:
Performance Rating
Effective Date
This enables deeper analysis and customized reporting.
